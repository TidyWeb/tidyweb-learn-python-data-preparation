Block 9 - ETL Drill

Block 9 turns the earlier cleaning moves into an ETL workflow: extract the data, transform it safely, then load the cleaned result. The notebook runs the pattern first on a dummy stock table and then repeats it as a timed drill on Seattle weather data.

NOTE 01

The ETL Pattern

Plain Englishextract transform load

Extract means get the data into the notebook. Transform means clean, reshape, rename, mask, or add columns. Load means save the cleaned result somewhere useful.

ETL is not a new kind of pandas command. It is an order of work.
